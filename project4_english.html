Finance & Insurance

Why and how should we teach children about finances?

About the project

The financial problems of the parents affect the children both socially as their mental health, in addition to the lack of knowledge about the the issue affects them as adults too. The teaching methodology for children is very different from that of adults, children need visual stimulation, colors and shapes of learning that involve "do activities in their daily life" so that they learn much better and faster. The project, which was my first individual project that spanned a total of approximately two weeks, was focused on a problem as it is that parents need to teach their children finances from an early age so that this benefits you both short and long term and not necessarily done.
