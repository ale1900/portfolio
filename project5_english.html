Pastry entrepreneurs

How can we help a pastry chef who is going to start his own business, that he does not know what to buy or where to start?

About the project

In this project I wanted to focus mainly on the pastry chef who will start a business and who is in the process of ignorance towards everything he must acquire to open his bakery to the public Things like machinery, ingredients, utensils etc There are many resources on the internet that could be used in this regard to investigate what would it take to open a pastry?, however the information is scattered, varied and global, so how can we solve this problem for the entrepreneur?

Use case

A use case is a description of how a person who actually use that process or system will accomplish a goal. can be used in reference to any process. For example, imagine you are a cook who has the objective of preparing a cheese sandwich a la grill. The use case would describe through a series of steps written how the cook would prepare that sandwich. A use case helps understand where errors might occur in the process and the design functions to resolve those errors.
